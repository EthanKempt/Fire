Why a Website?

We wanted to make it easier to access all your Assassins information in one place. This website is specifically built to work with instagram to make the game simpler and more fun. We also wanted to create a way to see pot value and other information like how many teams are left. Most importantly, we created a clear ruleset so that no one is getting screwed over in their game. If the game hasn't started yet, you can preview all the pages we've made below, or if the game has started, just log in.

This website has taken hours upon hours to build and despite our best efforts it's still pretty buggy. If you have any suggestions, feedback, or questions, email us at higleyassassins@gmail.com or message our Instaram here.

What about instagram?

Don't worry! You'll still get your assignments and be able to talk to us through instagram. This website is just allowing for those without instagram to play and, more importantly, make sure all the games information is made public. Basically if your set on using instagram, you can still play, you just miss out on the benfiits of the website.

Whenever you get out your targets, you'll still post the video on instagram and we'll repost it. We're also currently working on a way to automatically sync those videos to this page so you have them no matter where you go.
