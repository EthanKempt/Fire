Why a Website?

We wanted to make it easier to access all your Assassins information in one place. This website is specifically built to work with Instagram to make the game simpler and more fun. We also wanted to create a way to see information like pot value and how many teams are left. Most importantly, we created a clear rule set so that no one is getting screwed over in their game. If the game hasn't started yet, you can preview all the pages we've made below. Once it has, just log in.

This website has taken hours upon hours to build and despite our best efforts it's still somewhat buggy. If you have any suggestions, feedback, or questions, email us at higleyassassins@gmail.com or message our Instagram here.

What about Instagram?

Don't worry! You'll still get your assignments and be able to talk to us through Instagram. This website is just allowing for those without Instagram to play and, more importantly, make sure all the game's information is made public. Basically, if you're set on using Instagram, you can still play, you just miss out on the benefits of the website.

Whenever you get your targets out, you'll still post the video on Instagram and we'll re-post it. We're also currently working on a way to automatically sync those videos to this page so you have them no matter where you go.

How do I sign up?

Sign ups start the first week after break (Tuesday, January 2) and end the following week (Tuesday, January 9). In order to join you must pay $10 a person and have 3-4 people to create a team. You team MUST pay all at once through one of two methods:

  1. Pay in cash to either Ethan Kempton, Carson Wengert, or Mikenna Sharp. With your cash include a paper with your team name, player names (first and last name), and one players Instagram.
  2. Pay on Venmo. Venmo @ethankempton and in the description, put your team name, player names (first and last name) and one players Instagram.
Single Player/Duos

Many people want to play assassins but don't necessarily have a full group to join with. This is why we are allowing students to pay solo or in a pair and we'll put you on a team of other soloists. After the game starts there will be no difference between a soloist team and a normal team. If you are interested in joining this way, just pay by yourself or with one other person and in place of your team name, just write 'Single' or 'Double'. Make sure to provide your Instagram as this is our only way of communicating with you.

If you are a soloist you will not be placed on a team of 3. If you join as a soloist/duo and don't like your team, we unfortunately can't give you a refund. After your team is made, we'll put you in contact with your team members through Instagram.
